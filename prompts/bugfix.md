## Bug Fix Workflow

### Step 1

Describe the observed problem.

### Step 2

Collect evidence before modifying code.

### Step 3

Identify possible root causes.

Rank them by probability.

### Step 4

Implement the smallest reasonable fix.

### Step 5

Verify:

- Original bug resolved
- No regression
- No unrelated behavior changes

### Output

Always include:

Bug

↓

Root Cause

↓

Fix

↓

Risk

↓

Verification

↓

Remaining Questions

Never retry the same approach indefinitely.
